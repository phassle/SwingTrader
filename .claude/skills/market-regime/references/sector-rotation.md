# Sector Rotation by Economic Phase

## Phase 1: Early Recovery
**Leaders:** Financials, Discretionary, Real Estate
- Yield curve steepening, loan growth
- Swing trade: Breakouts in bank stocks after long bases.

## Phase 2: Mid-Cycle Expansion
**Leaders:** Technology, Industrials, Comms
- Strong earnings, capex spending
- Swing trade: Momentum/RS in tech, avoid rate-sensitive sectors.

## Phase 3: Late Cycle
**Leaders:** Energy, Materials, Healthcare, Staples
- Inflation, margin pressure
- Swing trade: Shorter holds, tighter stops, shift to value cyclicals.

## Phase 4: Recession/Contraction
**Leaders:** Utilities, Staples, Treasuries
- Defensive safety, dividend yield
- Swing trade: Mean reversion oversold bounces only; reduce leverage.

## Monthly Tactic

Check: Which 2-3 sectors are in relative strength uptrend vs OMXS30? Concentrate long swings there. Avoid choppy activity in lagging sectors.
