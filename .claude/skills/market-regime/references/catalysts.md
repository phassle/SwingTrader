# Catalyst Event Classification

## Quality Levels

| Quality Level | Examples | Swing Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **High** | Earnings beat+raise, major M&A filing, Riksbanken surprise | Hold through event; wider gap expected |
| **Medium** | Analyst upgrade, conference talk, large customer news | Avoid holding; tight stops |
| **Low** | Rumor, social-media hype | Ignore; high whipsaw risk |

## Rules

- Tag every setup with catalyst metadata.
- A breakout 1 hour before earnings is Regime D risk, not Regime A confidence.
- High-quality catalysts justify wider stops and overnight holds.
- Low-quality catalysts should never override technical signals.

## Swedish Market Calendar Events

- **Riksbanken monetary policy:** ~6x/year (Feb, Apr, Jun, Sep, Nov, Dec). Risk: Large gaps day-of if surprise.
- **Reporting season:** Clustered April-May (Q1), Oct-Nov (Q3).
- **Ex-dividend clusters:** April-May heavy for Swedish large-cap. Defensive names rally before ex-date, fade after.
- **EU data:** ECB rate calls affect OMX via EUR.
